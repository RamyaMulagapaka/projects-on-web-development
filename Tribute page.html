Tribute to A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

About A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

A.P.J.Abdul Kalam ,the "Missile Man of India" ,was born on october 15, 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He served as the 11th President of India and was a renowed scientist , known for his work in India's space and missile programs

Achievements

  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle (S.L.V.III)
  • Led India's successful nuclear tests in 1998
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "India 2020"
  • Recieved the Bharath Ratna ,India's highest civilian award
